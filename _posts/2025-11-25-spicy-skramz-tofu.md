### Ingredients
- 200g firm tofu, cubed  
- 1 cup black beans  
- 1/2 cup sauerkraut  
- 2 tbsp soy sauce  
- 1 tbsp maple syrup  
- 1 tsp sriracha (adjust to taste)  
- 1 tbsp nutritional yeast  

### Instructions
1. Heat a pan over medium heat. Add tofu and lightly brown.  
2. Add black beans and soy sauce, stir well.  
3. Mix in maple syrup, sriracha, and nutritional yeast.  
4. Serve topped with sauerkraut for that perfect tangy bite.
